Journaling Guide: Step by Step Process

Writing a journal takes a step by step process by first gathering your materials and choosing an environment that you are comfort with. Next begin with an initial warm up and focus on connection with your own thoughts and emotions.

1

Choose your materials and environment

First gather your journaling materials and choose a comfortable space where you can focus on writing.

2

Begin with a initial warm up

Begin your journaling with an initial warm up to ease you into the writing process by clearing your mind. Next start connecting with your thoughts and emotions in order to develop a more in-depth experience.

3

Choose a topic or journal prompt

Next, choose a topic or theme that resonates with you to write about. This will allow you to reflect on your personal growth and make some self-discoveries.

4

Write freely

Finally, writing freely without any judgment and expectations allows your thoughts to flow on the page. This will create some insight of a deeper understanding of your emotions and experiences. This will also allow you to discover parts of yourself that are hidden.

5

Reflect on your writing

After writing your journal entry, take a moment to consider any patterns of recognition or emotions that came up when writing. This will motivate you to identify areas of personal growth or answers to problems that you might be experiencing.
